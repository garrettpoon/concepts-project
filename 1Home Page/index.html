About the Project



This project challenged us to reinterpret a familiar object - in our case, a pen - and apply it in many different ways to mean something else. As we explored different concepts, one of the themes that stuck out to us was that most of our ideas could be categorized into different eras of world history. We began to dive deeper into this idea and eventually came up with 6 distinct eraâ€™s: Prehistoric, The Ancient Era, The Medieval Era, The 20th Century, The Modern Era, and The Future. Within our project, the modern version of the pen transcends all of these eras in intriguing and humorous ways.
